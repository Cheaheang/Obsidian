To make good SEO, You need make user stay at your as long as possible 
**RULE:**
**CTR**(Click through rate) : Higher the better
**SERP**
**Bounce Rate**: lower the better
**Dwell Time**: the longer the better(How many time they spend on the website )

User sometime never click back or close the page, that why we keep track of **SESSION DURATION** and **PAGES PER SESSION**

**article** tell search engine that It the **main content**
**title** will display in the search page
**Outbound Links**: Signal what your content is about
